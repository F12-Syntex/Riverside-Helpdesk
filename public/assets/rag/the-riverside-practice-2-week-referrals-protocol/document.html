THE RIVERSIDE PRACTICE 2 WEEK REFERRALS PROTOCOL

PURPOSE

To ensure that all suspected 2 week referrals made by the practice are followed up to ensure:

  • No delay in diagnosis
  • Identify any GP actions or follow up
  • Good clinical governance by keeping accurate patient records of any outcomes and diagnoses
  • Maintain accurate cancer and if applicable palliative care registers
  • Timely review of a patient with a new cancer diagnosis
  • Follow up of patients who do not attend their appointment

APPLICABILTY

All staff involved in a 2 week referral including admin and doctors.

PROCESS

  • Referrers must complete the suspected cancer 2 week template logging the type of cancer suspected and ensure admin are informed live at the appointment
  • Admin to try and book the patient appointment live while at the surgery and inform the patient of the date and time.
  • Admin to enter in the patient notes and template the date of the appointment
  • Admin to maintain a list of all referrals, date of appointment, confirm if clinic letter received
  • GPs to code on the 2 week template if cancer was confirmed, and if the patient attended
  • GPs to code in the 2 week template and follow up if a patient DNA’s their hospital appointment

SAFETY NETTING

Admin to check WEEKLY the 2 week spreadsheet and chase up any outstanding clinic letters and inform the senior GP if any actions are required.
