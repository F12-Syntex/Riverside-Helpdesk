Clear Desk Policy

Policy

The Clear Desk and Screen Policy will support (name of practice) in preserving the confidentiality, integrity and availability of the NHS and other UK Government data manually handled and on IT systems used by (name of practice) and other third party staff.

Clear desk policy

  • At the end of the working day or when leaving the office, during the day, all documents or media should be secured in lockable commercial office furniture (desk drawers, filing cabinets etc.)
  • Removable media should be locked away
  • Personal belongings should be removed from view
  • Office/work area windows should be closed when working areas are unattended and at the end of the working day
  • All internal doors should be closed when working area are unattended and at the end of the working day
  • In ground floor work areas, blinds should be closed or PC/Laptop screen, information boards or any protectively marked or sensitive information shall positioned so it cannot be viewed by a passer-by
  • All desk pedestals should be locked away when working areas are unattended and at the end of the working day
  • All cabinets should be locked when working areas are unattended and at the end of the working day
  • All laptops should be secured in suitable containers when working areas are unattended and at the end of the working day
  • All printers should be cleared of printed material when working areas are unattended and at the end of the working day.
  • All photocopiers should be cleared of printed material when working areas are unattended and at the end of the working day
  • All white boards should be wiped clean when working areas are unattended and at the end of the working day
  • All flip charts shall be cleared of information when working areas are unattended and at the end of the working day

Clear screen policy

  • For all the riverside IT systems, computer screens should be angled away from the view of unauthorised persons
  • All users should ensure that any information that is confidential should not be overseen by those without a need to know
  • Screens shall be cleared or locked when talking to unauthorised persons
  • All computer screens should have the auto screen saver set to activate when there is no activity for a period, suggested as no longer than 15 minutes
  • Users should invoke the screen lockout for periods when they are away from their device for no longer than 45 minutes. For periods longer than 45 minutes and at the end of the working day they should log off or shut down the device and switch off the screen.
  • Users should be required to re-authenticate to unlock their screens.
