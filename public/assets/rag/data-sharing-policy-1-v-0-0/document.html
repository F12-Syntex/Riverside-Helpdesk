THE RIVERSIDE PRACTICE
Data Sharing Policy

By ‘data sharing’ we mean the disclosure of data from one or more
third party organisations or Hospitals. Data sharing can take the form of
fax or secure NHS net email.

Information requested can be of some or all of below:

Your NHS number
Date of birth.
Your address
Your gender and ethnicity.
The medical diagnoses on your record, and any referrals.
Prescriptions (past or present)

Patient data requests by Hospitals are emailed to the surgery to the generic email address nelondonicb.theriversidepractice@nhs.net; once the practice are sure that this is secure and on a NHS net email then the relevant information is emailed back. This is usually dealt with immediately to prevent any delay.

Solicitor’s requests are sent by letter or emailed to the practice with signed consent by the patient. Medical records are copied or medical reports are prepared and sent (special delivery) to the requester within a limited time and to comply with the requirements of the Data Protection Act 1998. They can also be sent securely by email using the IGPR software, which encrypts the data and is password protected and has been approved by the data protection officer for the ICB.
