THE RIVERSIDE PRACTICE
SYSTEM FOR CHECKING EMERGENCY DRUGS
Introduction

The purpose of this statement is to formally record the procedure for the checking of emergency drugs.

Routine stock checks of drug supplies should be planned and carried out during normal working hours.

This is to include a check of expiry dates of drugs held both on the premises and within doctors' bags. In the event of a CQC inspection visit, a random sample of drugs and bags may take place and doctors will be questioned on their procedures for ensuring compliance.
Procedure

The system is nurse/HCA -led.

Responsible person: Mishalu Joseph (PN).
Deputy when absent: Iqra Khan (HCA)

Drugs to be checked:

Emergency drug cupboard in Room 4 Treatment room Clearly indicated
GP bag Located in store room on second shelf on the RIGHT when entering room.
Emergency Drugs in all clinical room cupboards

Items to be checked:

Expiry date
Quantity remaining

The results of the check will be recorded in the record book which is held in black folder on the fridge in Treatment room 4. The check will take place at the beginning of every month.
