The Riverside Practice Stop Smoking Services

The practice actively encourages all of its patients who smoke to stop opportunistically when consulting with any of the clinical staff, HCA and at routine health checks. This is recorded in the patient's EMIS record using the lifestyle template including patient consent to be contacted by the local stop smoking service. Literature is provided in the surgery (in several languages) and a poster in the waiting room alerts patients to the service. This service is also promoted in the practice leaflet and patient information Jayex Screen.

The smoking status is entered on the CEG templates at the new patient check and also reviewed opportunistically at consultation with the nurse or doctor for existing patients. In those who smoke their intentions and past attempts to stop smoking are discussed and if they want to stop then a quit date is agreed and entered onto the CEG template.

If a patient is ready to stop then they are referred by EMAILING a signed referral form to the local City & Hackney stop smoking services. This offers nicotine replacement and level 2 and level 3 services.
